Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

At Daily Cleaning Services, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • Name and company name
  • Contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Location / site address
  • Interactions with us and service requests

2. How We Use Your Information

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • Provide you with quotes and our services
  • Communicate with you regarding scheduling and operations
  • Improve our services and customer experience

3. Data Protection

We keep your information safe by storing it in secure, encrypted systems and only allowing authorised staff to access it for the purpose of fulfilling operational duties.

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share necessary details with trusted subcontractors strictly for the purpose of completing the services you have requested (e.g., providing site addresses to our cleaning or devanning crews).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you'd like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at:

  • Email: enquiries@dailycleaning.co.nz
  • Phone: 0800 123 456
