About Daily Cleaning Services

We are a proudly Auckland-based operational support company, built on the foundations of hard work, reliability, and an uncompromising commitment to safety.

Our Story

Daily Cleaning Services began with a simple mission: to provide Auckland businesses with a single, dependable partner for their most pressing operational challenges.

What started as a dedicated commercial cleaning crew quickly expanded. Our clients in the logistics and warehousing sectors asked us to apply our rigorous, process-driven approach to other areas—leading to the launch of our Devanning, Staffing, and MPI Accredited Person services.

Today, we are the trusted backbone for numerous facilities across the region. When you hire us, you aren't just getting labour; you're getting a management team that cares about your deadlines and compliance as much as you do.

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by these three fundamental principles.

Safety First

We never compromise on health and safety. Rigorous training and strict protocols ensure our team and your site remain secure.

Unwavering Reliability

If we say we'll be there, we'll be there. We understand the cost of delays in the commercial sector.

Partnership Approach

We view ourselves as an extension of your business. Your success and operational fluidity is our primary metric.
