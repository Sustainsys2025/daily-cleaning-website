Logistics & Devanning

Fast, safe, and efficient container unloading to keep your supply chain moving without delays.

Unloading Expertise You Can Rely On

In the fast-paced world of logistics, delays at the loading dock disrupt the entire supply chain. Our devanning teams are trained for speed, accuracy, and absolute safety.

We handle everything from loose cartons to oversized, awkward freight. By outsourcing your container devanning to us, your core team can focus on distribution while we handle the heavy lifting.

Our Devanning Process:

Safe container door opening protocols

Careful hand-unloading and palletising

Shrink wrapping and labelling to your specifications

Stock sorting and basic quality checks upon arrival

Container sweeping and clean-out post devan

Strict adherence to Health & Safety manual handling guidelines

Combine this service with our MPI Accredited Person service for a seamless container arrival process.
